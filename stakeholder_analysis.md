# Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholders

| Name | Role | Interest | Influence |
|------|------|----------|-----------|
| John Doe | Product Owner | Ensures product meets market needs | High |
| Jane Smith | Business Analyst | Defines requirements and validates solutions | High |
| Development Team | Implementers | Builds the solution according to specs | Medium |
| End Users | Users | Uses the system to achieve business goals | High |
| Compliance Officer | Regulatory | Ensures compliance with legal standards | Medium |

## Stakeholder Concerns
- Alignment with business objectives
- Regulatory compliance
- Usability and performance
- Maintenance and support
