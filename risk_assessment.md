# Risk Assessment & Mitigation

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Impact | Probability | Mitigation Strategy |
|---------|------------------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| **R-001** | **Data Breach**<br>Unauthorized access to stored documents. | High | Low | Implement strong encryption at rest and in transit. Regular security audits. |
| **R-002** | **Service Downtime**<br>System becomes unavailable during critical sharing times. | High | Low | Use reliable cloud hosting with redundancy. Implement monitoring and alerts. |
| **R-003** | **Lost Access Codes**<br>Users lose the codes needed to access shared documents. | Medium | Medium | Provide a "Resend Access Code" feature for document owners. |
| **R-004** | **Malicious Uploads**<br>Users uploading viruses or malware. | High | Low | Integrate virus scanning on upload. Restrict file types to safe formats (PDF, IMG). |
| **R-005** | **Low Adoption**<br>Users find the QR process too complex. | Medium | Medium | Focus on UX design. Provide clear, step-by-step tutorials. |
